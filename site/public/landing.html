Features

No server needed

Using SoStatic removes the need for developing, hosting and maintaining your own servers just for the simple task of receiving form submissions

Zero dependencies

Our solution uses the native HTML form submission process. The only library you will need to add is Google's ReCaptcha, if you decide to use it for spam filtering

ReCaptcha support

With just a checkbox, you can integrate spam filtering on the using Google's recaptcha for free.

Hide your email

Using our service, you wont have to make your form processing email public to the visitors

Easy setup

Adding SoStatic to your website is as easy as replacing the Action parameter on your form

Easy updates

You can change the behaviour of your form processing without needing to redeploy your website

GDPR compliant

We implement all the required mechanism to be compliant with GDPR and will guide you through adding them to your website as well

Secure

All communication can be done through SSL just by adding an 's' to the link we provide ;)

Pricing

Free for up to 100 valid messages / month

We understand that most small websites wont get more than a few contact messages per month. We offer our service for free for websites that receive less than 100 valid messages per month. A message is considered valid if the recaptcha was succesfully filled. Spam messages will not count towards your quota

More than 100 valid messages per month?

The messages will not be lost, but they will no longer be forwarded to your email, they will only be accessible via our platform. If you find value in having all messages forwarded, we can setup a recurring payment method for 5$/month for unlimited messages per month.
