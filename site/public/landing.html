Pricing

Free for up to 100 valid messages / month

We understand that most small websites wont get more than a few contact messages per month. We offer our service for free for websites that receive less than 100 valid messages per month. A message is considered valid if the recaptcha was succesfully filled. Spam messages will not count towards your quota

What if you get over 100 valid messages per month?

The messages will not be lost, but they will no longer be forwarded to your email, they will only be accessible via our platform. If you find value in having all messages forwarded, we can setup a recurring payment method for 5$/month for unlimited messages per month.
